Research Activities

Study of graphs and their applications in chemistry, focusing on molecular structures and connectivity indices.

Mathematical descriptors of molecular structures used in QSAR and QSPR modeling.

Application of computational techniques to study molecular behavior and chemical properties.

Developing Python-based tools for graph analysis and molecular modeling.

Received the prestigious Raman Research Award three times from Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) in recognition of outstanding research contributions published in high-impact Scopus-indexed journals. This award honors excellence in scholarly publications and significant contributions to the advancement of research.

Received the Best Paper Award at the International Conference on Graph Theory (ICGT 2024), held on January 8-9, 2024, at Periyar University, Salem, for the research on "Generalized Results for Degree-Based Entropies of Aluminosilicate Minerals and Their Applications in QSPR Modeling."

Fourth International Conference on Mathematics and Statistics (AUS-ICMS’25), American University of Sharjah, UAE.

Discussed advancements in graph-based molecular descriptors.

Presented novel approaches to molecular topology computation.
